Parameters to Calculate Green Score

  • Air Quality Index

    Air quality, including SO2, NO2, and RSPM affects health and planning.

  • Temperature

    Weather data includes temperature, humidity, precipitation, wind, and solar conditions.

  • Water Spread

    Water body data includes type, depth, and storage capacity details.

  • Soil

    Soil quality, including moisture levels, is vital for plant growth.

  • Forest Cover

    Forest cover data includes types, area percentages, and changes.

  • Population

    Population growth data includes growth rate and district-specific figures.

Odisha Green Score

Urban Biome offers a revolutionary solution for optimizing green space placement in cities. Our cloud-based platform combines GIS data, machine learning, and community input to generate comprehensive "Green Scores" for potential sites. By addressing key challenges in urban planning—such as environmental suitability assessment, data integration, and visualization—Urban Biome empowers planners to make informed decisions quickly and efficiently. Our scalable, AI-driven approach not only saves time and resources but also ensures that green spaces align with both environmental needs and community preferences. As cities worldwide prioritize sustainability and livability, Urban Biome stands poised to become an indispensable tool in modern urban development.
